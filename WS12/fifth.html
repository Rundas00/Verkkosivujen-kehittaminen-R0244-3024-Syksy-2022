Ancients

Ancients or The Ancients, also known as Ancestors/The Ancestors, Lanteans, and Gate Builders, or Anquietas in their language, are ethereal ascended beings with near infinite knowledge and powerful abilities. The Ancients are descended from those Alterans that fled the Ori in a distant galaxy, and were one of the most advanced races known to have existed in the Milky Way, and reigned for millions of years as a secular and science-oriented power, reaching their level of technology long before the humans evolved on Earth and are known as the builders of the Stargates. They follow a rule of non-interference, because the temptation to manipulate and align lower life forms in some order for one self purposes could result in this type of abusive corruption and have a great place in the mythologies and cultures of other civilisations.
