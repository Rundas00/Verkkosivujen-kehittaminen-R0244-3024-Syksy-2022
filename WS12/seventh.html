Ori

are extinct ethereal ascended Alterans who had near infinite knowledge and powerful abilities that first evolved as a physical species from the Alteran Home Galaxy. The Ori, before ascending, emerged from Celestis. The Ori used their knowledge and power as a justification and a means to demand the worship of mortal beings, when they learned such worship increases the power of ascended beings. They were destroyed by the Sangraal as the Great Enlightenment drew to it end.
