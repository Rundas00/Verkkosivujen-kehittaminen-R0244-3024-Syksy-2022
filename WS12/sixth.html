Wraiths

The Wraith are a humanoid hive-based species from Pegasus, a satellite galaxy of the Milky Way, who harvest the 'life-force' of other humanoid beings for nourishment through suckers on their right hand palm. Countless worlds live in constant fear of the Wraith, who return periodically to cull their human herds. After taking their fill, the Wraith hibernate for centuries, watched over by Keepers, before they wake and feed again.
